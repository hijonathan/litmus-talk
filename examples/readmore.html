Introducing the Wistia Learning Center!
Videos about video to help you market with video.
Read more right from your email.

Here at Wistia, we want everyone to be able to succeed at marketing with web video. In support of that goal, today we're launching the Wistia Learning Center, a new, dedicated area for video tips, tricks, ideas, and inspiration.

We'd love nothing more than to be able to tell you, "just start using Wistia and you'll immediately morph into a web video superstar." But realistically, video marketing can be tough - there's concepting, production, measurement, and lots more to consider. We've been at it for 5 years and we still have tons to learn. The good news is that we've been documenting what we've figured out along the way, and now we have just the place to share what we learned.

Our blog will always be a place for us to share new information and explore topics that interest us. The purpose of creating a separate Learning Center is to have a more organized way for you to access our video resources (and find the ones that are most relevant to you).

The Wistia Learning Center is a collection of videos about video. That you learned about through a blog post about blog posts about videos about video. And we'll be adding new videos all the time.

What's in the Wistia Learning Center now?

An organized collection of our tips and tricks for better business video Two brand new videos (Analytics for Testimonials and Make 5 Videos, Not 1) (and a couple others you might not have seen!) Let us know what you think -- and what you'd like to see us cover more of in the future!
